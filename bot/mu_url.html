Good Start   
Rating: 7.5 / 10.0
by great_k
March 18th, 2015, 4:22am
I quite like the MC. He is an excellent fighter who quickly adapts to the game and develops his own unique abilities.
The rest of the cast has been pretty dull. The story itself is getting quite repetitive, where our MC keeps finding himself targeted by the western conqueror and his guild.
To those that say the pace of the manga is too fast, if the manga pace where any slower it would be painful. There is no way I could read the LN if the pace is that slow. At ch11 the MC has not joined any guilds, nor has he met up with the girl he is supposed to be a bodyguard for. My biggest complaint so far is how little has happened.
However it is still early days. We have a cool MC and a story that could go anywhere so I am optimistic about the future of this series.
